CRITICAL NOTE ON VASP PSEUDOPOTENTIALS (POTCAR):
Due to licensing and copyright restrictions enforced by the VASP Software GmbH (University of Vienna), original POTCAR files cannot be distributed in public GitHub repositories.

To reproduce these calculations, please concatenate the standard PBE PAW pseudopotentials from your licensed VASP distribution in the following order:
1. Ti_pv (Titanium with p and d valence states)
2. O     (Oxygen standard)
3. C     (Carbon standard for Graphene monolayer)
